Message from Management

CONCEPTA CONTRACTING SERVICES - QATAR, CCS is a newly established contracting company in Qatar, gathering a team with past knowledge in residential, Oil & Gas, industrial as well as commercial projects in the Gulf and the Middle-East.

CCS team has a vast experience across sectors, ranging from construction, finance and real estate, which they will invest to contribute to the economic advancement and growth of the State of Qatar.

The integrity and excellence of our work have been verified in many prestigious projectsin both the Public and Private sectors. The CCS team has a remarkably rich experience in the construction industry on Commercial and Residential buildings, Infrastructure Works, Oil & Gas, Pre-Engineered buildings, Pre-Fabricated Housing, Electro-mechanical Works, as well as Industrial Coating & Insulation Works.

The CCS team gathers dedicated, well-organized and highly experienced professionals who ensure highest quality standards are timely delivered to our clients. Our expert team of engineers provides Design, Quantity Surveying, Estimates, BOQs, Specifications, Drawings and Schedules.
